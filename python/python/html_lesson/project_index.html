History of Biriyani(The Food we love most!):

The first Biryani is said to have been made in Persia, where meat and rice were cooked together. Later, this dish was introduced to India by the Mughals, where it was adapted to suit local palates and cooking styles. The result, today's delicious Biryani, was born!(Source: THE HISTORY OF BIRYANI, AN ANCIENT DISH THAT IS MAKING ITS MODERN COMEBACK By saadat

According to historian Lizzie Collingham, the modern biryani developed in the royal kitchens of the Mughal Empire (1526-1857) and is a mix of the native spicy rice dishes of India and the Persian pilaf. (Source: wikipedia
